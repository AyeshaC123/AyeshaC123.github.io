Topic: The Role of Tech within LGBTQ+ education within K-12

My Driving Question:

The Driving Question for my project is: In what ways can technology be leveraged to support LGBTQ+ youth in educational settings, and what challenges must be addressed to ensure it serves as a positive force?

How can digital platforms, educational apps, and social media be utilized to foster a sense of belonging and community for LGBTQ+ students, while also navigating concerns around privacy, cyberbullying, and access?

By: Ayesha Chaudhry

A Web Application developed by me using html and javascript that acts as a daily calendar to provide information on how technology can be utilized to help educated K-12 students on LGBTQ+ topics and issues.
