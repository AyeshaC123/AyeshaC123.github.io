Challenges in Implementing technology within LGBTQ+ education

There come some challenges with integrating technology into the LGBTQ+ curriculum. One of these challenges include that with OER gaining popularity in the K-12 community, teachers have expressed that they feel that if they publish OER on LGBTQ+ content, these resources will end up being accessed by the wrong people and changed in a way that can be harmful. Harmful to the students and the teachers who are creating it.
