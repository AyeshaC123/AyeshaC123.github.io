Advantages in implementing technology to support LGBTQ+ education

Currently the only feasible option for materials and tools to use for LGBTQ+ education include textbooks and in person interviews. Digital materials are easily accessible, transportable and easier for teachers to gear their lesson plans around.

Textbooks can get outdated very easily. “A lot of schools are using ten, fifteen, twenty-year old textbooks. And for talking about queer and trans identities, our understanding of these identities evolves. And our language for understanding these identities evolves.” Sabia Prescott said. Digital materials is more current, and the opportunity to update and revise the content is a huge advantage to OER.
