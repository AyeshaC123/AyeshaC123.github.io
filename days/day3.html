Day 3: Introduction to Aroma Therapy

Sabia Prescott

Sabia Prescott, a senior program associate for the New American Foundation spent two years looking for OER materials meant for LGBTQ students and found absolutely nothing. This has always been the case despite several states such as California, New Jersey, Colorado, and Illinois have rules that require LGBTQ+ inclusion in pre K-12 curriculum.

This lack of resources persists despite legislative mandates in several states—including California, New Jersey, Colorado, and Illinois—that require the inclusion of LGBTQ+ content in the curricula of pre-kindergarten through 12th grade. These mandates aim to promote inclusivity and representation in educational materials, yet the absence of suitable OER materials highlights a significant gap in meeting these legislative goals.
